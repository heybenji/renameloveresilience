Platonic Life Partner Book Project

We're Amelia Ortega and Benji Mauer. We met 19 years years ago, and over the years have come to think of each other as platonic life partners and family. Nine years ago we made the decision to publicly acknowledge our relationship in a community event of love and commitment titled “Rename Love Resilience.” ( link to commitment statement is below)

We believe that relationships find their own truth and reason for being. As we approach our 20 year anniversary, we'd like to gather people again. This time, we're seeking narratives, encounters, memories, artifacts, photographs, and creative works about platonic life partnerships, best friends, queer family, commitments formed outside of traditional marriages.

Call for Submissions

We are seeking a diverse body of contributions to this anthology on Platonic Life Partnership and chosen families. Queer families and chosen families are a function of survival, of generational commitment, of social and cultural place making. We welcome you to contribute creative works that speak to the ways in which love, connection, family and platonic partnership have touched your life.

This anthology will serve as an archive for relationships that once were, that continue to be, that are seeking to become.

We encourage you to consider:

  • How have platonic relationships have supported you?
  • What are some commitments you've made to friends or chosen family?
  • What possibilities do platonic life partnerships hold for you?
  • How do non-state controlled relationships work to subvert the power structures and systems that inform the panopticon of capitalist patriarchy under the current administration?
  • What un-told stories are there in your own families regarding chosen partners and family that have existed outside of the institution of marriage?
